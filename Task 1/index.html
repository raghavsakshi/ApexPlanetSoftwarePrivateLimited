NEW RELEASES

Her

"Her" is a sci-fi romantic drama about a lonely man who falls in love with an intelligent operating system, exploring themes of love, technology, and human connection.

Star Wars

"Star Wars" is an epic space fantasy saga that follows the battle between good and evil in a galaxy far, far away, featuring Jedi, Sith, the Force, and iconic characters like Luke Skywalker, Darth Vader, and Princess Leia.

Storm

A dark urban fantasy where a mans life is turned upside down by a mysterious woman and a strange box.

1917

"1917" is a World War I epic about two British soldiers sent on a dangerous mission to deliver a message that could save 1,600 lives.

Avengers

"The Avengers" is a superhero action series where Marvel heroes like Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and others team up to fight powerful threats and save the world.

Rampagne

A primatologist must team up with a giant, mutated albino gorilla to stop other monstrous creatures from destroying the city after a genetic experiment goes wrong.

Lego Batman

In this animated comedy, LEGO Batman faces his greatest fear—having a family—while battling villains like the Joker. Full of humor, heart, and action, it's a fun twist on the Batman legend.

1920 London

"1920 London" is a supernatural horror film where a woman seeks help from her ex-lover, an exorcist, to save her husband who is possessed by a dark spirit. Set against the eerie backdrop of 1920s London, the film blends love, revenge, and black magic.

Ender's Game

In a future where Earth is under threat from alien invaders, a gifted boy named Ender Wiggin is recruited into a military training program to prepare for a space war. He must lead the fight to save humanity.

Oblivion

In a post-apocalyptic future, a drone repairman discovers shocking secrets about the war-torn Earth, his mission, and his identity—leading him to question everything he knows.

Hobbit

"The Hobbit" follows the journey of Bilbo Baggins, a reluctant hobbit who joins a group of dwarves and the wizard Gandalf to reclaim a lost kingdom from the fearsome dragon Smaug. Along the way, Bilbo finds a mysterious ring that changes the fate of Middle-earth.

Due date

A stressed-out father-to-be must get home in time for his child's birth—but ends up on a wild cross-country road trip with an unpredictable and annoying travel companion.

kick ass2

In this action-comedy sequel, amateur superhero Kick-Ass joins a team of masked vigilantes led by Colonel Stars and Stripes. Meanwhile, Hit-Girl tries to live a normal life, and a new villain rises to seek revenge for his father’s death.

The Emoji Movie

Inside a smartphone, a “meh” emoji named Gene is born with multiple expressions. To become "normal," he embarks on an app-hopping adventure with his friends to find his true purpose and save their digital world.

1920

A newlywed couple moves into a haunted mansion in 1920s India. Soon, the wife becomes possessed by a dark spirit, and her husband must fight to save her soul through love, faith, and exorcism.

Paths of glory

During World War I, a French army officer defends three innocent soldiers accused of cowardice after a failed and deadly mission. The film powerfully explores military injustice, honor, and the futility of war.
